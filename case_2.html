Designing a simple onboarding that keeps creators motivated, encouraged, and committed to their creator journey.

Project Brief

Onboarding: We want creators who sign up for vidIQ to feel motivated, encouraged, and committed to their creator journey. Our current onboarding process is pretty simple (or non-existent), and doesn’t build a relationship with the creator.

You should design an empathetic onboarding flow that >connects with the creator on a personal level to increase motivation and commitment before they embark on their long and difficult journey of being a content creator.

Research

Before starting out, I looked through the existing onboarding on VidIQ and took note of what works and what can be done better on the flow by making the onboarding experience more personalised and building a relationship with each creator. Different creators have different goals for using the VidIQ app and it is important to understand each user’s unique need and tailor it to VidIQ’s offerings.

I also went ahead to take note of competitors (tubebuddy) and products that need to connect with their users on a personal level: spotify(creating playlists that matches each user’s preference), headspace(finding out each user’s unique welness goal).

Solution

The new flow will capture some of the existing features from the current VidIQ Onboarding. It also captures finding out each user’s short term goal and helping them set it so, they can always be reminded hours before to start planning towards it.

From my research, I found out that the major solution to creating great relationships with each users is asking them questions that are “user centered”. Asking users about their needs.

To keep them motivated and committed: There will be an incentive after they achieve this goal

Setting this goal will not be mandatory, they can simply skip the step and go to their dashboard and this is because I want to give the users the choice to do this now or later. >

Other Considerations

The users will be able to track the goal set during their onboarding and set new goals once they are in the app (the design does not capture this, it’s just an idea from the other task).
