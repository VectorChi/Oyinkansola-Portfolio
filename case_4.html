Overdraft Feature (Web)

The overdraft feature was designed to help the bank’s customers easily access credit (overdraft) at any point in time without providing any paperwork.

About Kuda Bank

Kuda Bank is a Neo-Bank founded in 2016 with over 4 million customers. Kuda's mission is to bank the unbanked and underbanked Africans wherever they might be in the world.

Over the past 3 years, we have built checking and savings products, the next phase of this project is credit. Credit not only aligns with the vision of the company, but it is a key revenue driver for the business.

The objective of this project is to offer a line of credit to eligible Kuda users via an overdraft. The Kuda overdraft will allow users drawn down on their account to certain predefined amount.

Problem Statement

As a customer using a bank trying to make an urgent payment or purchase but does not have sufficient funds, you might get frustrated that you cannot make this payment and look for ways to borrow as quick as possible.

How Might We?

Give Kuda customers the ability to access credit when they have insufficient funds.

Research and Discovery

A research was carried out to find out if users are familiar with overdrafts and if it is a feature they will be interested in. Here are some of the key findings:

Most users do not understand how overdraft works and cannot compare it with traditional loans.
With other banking platforms, the Overdraft amount offered to some customers are lower than what they need.

Solution

The solution will be a credit feature called overdraft which users can activate without any paperwork but users must meet certain eligibility requirements (depending on how often they transact on Kuda).

Users will have access to a specific amount as overdraft which can increase if the user’s monthly transaction rate increases.

Business Impact

Overdrafts repayment come with a daily interest rate and this can be a revenue channel for the business.

This feature will also enable growth as there will be new accounts from users trying to access the overdraft feature.

Onboarding

The users are educated about Overdraft and how the feature works.

Activating Overdraft

Users can activate their overdraft if they wish to. After activating overdraft, users can change the limit of overdraft they want to have access to, so they will not be able to spend above that limit.

Canceling Overdraft

Users can cancel their overdraft only after they have repaid their used overdraft.

Unable to Cancel Overdraft

Users cannot cancel their overdraft if they have not repaid their used overdraft.

Overdraft Repayment

Overdrafts get repaid once the user gets funded on their account. Once overdraft has been repaid, the user sees this pop up modal on their next log in to their account.

Overdraft Explainer

The overdraft explainer is a part of the overdraft dashboard so users can differentiate overdrafts from loans.

Success Metric

We tested the new iteration with the Customer Operations team and the task completion rate was higher than the first iteration.

Conclusion

The Overdraft feature on Kuda app is one the features that has increased the revenue of the company tremendously as there are daily interest rates on overdrafts and the feature has also helped the customer base experience growth. Users sign up on the app to partake of the credit feature.
