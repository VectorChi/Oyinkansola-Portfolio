About Kuda Bank

Kuda Bank is a Neo-Bank founded in 2016 with over 4 million customers. Kuda's mission is to bank the unbanked and underbanked Africans wherever they might be in the world.

Over the past 3 years, we have built checking and savings products, the next phase of this project is credit. Credit not only aligns with the vision of the company, but it is a key revenue driver for the business.

The objective of this project is to help the internal users respond to loan requests with no hassle and effectively.

Project Summary

The internal users (staff in Kuda) handle all administrative functionalities on Kuda Suites (Internal tool). On the banking application, a new feature called “Salary Loans” was recently developed for working-class customers. Therefore, the internal team will need to provision administrative functionalities for the “Salary Loans” feature on Kuda Suites.

How Might We?

Improve internal efficiency for Suites users while attending salary loan requests from customers.

Solution

The solution will be a new module called the Credit Management Module and the sub-module will be called Salary Loans Management”. In the future, there willl be considerations for other Sub-modules but we will be focused only on salary loans for this project.

Customer experience officers and the credit officers will be granted access to this module so they can review requests and approve or reject them with reasons. Suites has a maker-checker functionality (requires two-level approval before a request is completed).

The Requester: Reviews documents and loan requests, then accepts or rejects with a reason. Accepted requests moves to the approver for final review.

The Approver: Gives the accepted requests a final look, the accept or rejects for second review

Potential Impact

Creating a seamless experience for users to attend to customer loans requests will increase the effeciency of loans being given out and loans repayment come with an interest rate which can be a revenue channel for the business.

First Design Iteration

Since Suites is composed of different modules and sub-modules, we created a new module for Credit features (Overdrafts, salary loans and term loans). We then designed a sub-module for salary loans, this is where all the salary loans requests will be treated by Customer operation officers and approved by credit officers. The documents will be reviewed as wel as the loan details before a decision is made.

Reviewing Loan Requests

There is a logged table of pending requests where the users can review one after the other, then sent to the approver for final review.

Second Design Iteration

We decided to keep the table but add some flexibilty for the users after reviewing requests.

Testing Review Loan Iteration

After testing the previous design with the CX Officers, we realised the process was a little bit unecessarily long. We decided to add an action to move to next request or previous request and once the user is done treating one request, they automatically see the next request instead of going back to the logged table. This makes the review process faster and more efficient.

Book loan after offer has been signed

The customers get an email with a loan offer once their loan request has been accepted. After they sign and send back, their loan will then be booked.

Approver Dashboard

The approver receives the requests that have been reviewed by the requester for final approval.

Approver Dashboard

Suites is built with a maker-checker functionality, so the approver needs to approve the reviewed request. The customer is then sent an email with the loan offer after which it is processed after it has been signe dby the customer.

Success Metric

We tested the new iteration with the Customer Operations team and the task completion rate was higher than the first iteration.

Conclusion

The Credit Management Module was built majorly to help improve efficiency with how the loans requests are reviewed and attended to.
